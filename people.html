Team Flash

Barry Allen

Barry Allen also known as The Flash is a Forensic Scientist from Central City who works for The Central City Police Department. His parents are Henry Allen and the late Nora Allen. He is also a Metahuman and one of the first of his kind, having received unique powers, most notably Superhuman Speed.
