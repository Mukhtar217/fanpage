Team Flash

Barry Allen also known as The Flash is a Forensic Scientist from Central City who works for The Central City Police Department. His parents are Henry Allen and the late Nora Allen. He is also a Metahuman and one of the first of his kind, having received unique powers, most notably Superhuman Speed.

Cisco Ramon Born Francisco Ramon also known as Vibe is an expert mechanical engineering genius who works for S.T.A.R. Labs Central City Branch.

Caitlin Snow also known as Killer Frost is a highly intelligent bio engineering expert scientist who works at S.T.A.R. Labs Central City Branch. She is also from Central City as well she recently lost her fiance Ronnie Raymond during the recent explosion at S.T.A.R. Labs with The Particle Accelerator.

Joe West is the Father of Iris West and the Adoptive Father of Barry Allen. He's also a Detective of the Central City Police Department

Harrison Wells form Earth 2, also known as Harry is the father of Jesse Quick. He recently traveled through The Multiverse Breach currently connecting Earth 2 and Earth 1 and joined forces with The Flash and his Team in battling Zoom and saving his Daughter and his own world from the threat of Zoom.

Iris West is the daughter of Joe West and the late Francine West an older sister of Wally West. She is a reporter for Central City Picture News and the Wife of Barry Allen
