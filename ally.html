Allies

Team Arrow is a vigilante team dedicated to fighting criminals, mainly operating in Star City.

Supergirl is an alien from Earth-38. Strong, bulletproof, and friendly. Flash meets supergirl when he entered her Earth by accident.

A group of miscasts come together to become Legends. The Legends have the abilty to travel anywhere in time and have the job to protect the timeline.

Leonard Snart also known as Captain Cold is well known and highly skilled professional thief of Central City. His partner is Heat Wave. He is the son of a Former Bent Central City Cop and older brother of Lisa Snart. At a young age he learned to study the police to make sure he always got away before they could respond.

Ronald "Ronnie" Raymond also known as Firestorm was Mechanical Engineer who worked at S.T.A.R. Labs in Central City. He was engaged to be married to his fiance Caitlin Snow another Team Member of S.T.A.R. Labs.

Jesse Chambers Wells also known Jesse Quick is a Metahuman Speedster from Earth 2. She is from Central City and is The Daughter of Harrison Wells

Wallace West also known as Kid Flash is a Metahuman Speedster. He is also the Son of Joe West and Francine West. He is the younger brother of Iris West.
