Main Villains

Born in the Year 2151, Eobard Thawne also known as The Reverse Flash is a Mysterious and Extremely Dangerous Metahuman Speedster with a Former Connection to The Speed Force which is now only temporary and extremely knowledgeable of it's Powers. He is the Archenemy of The Flash and one of heroes most well known Villains

Hunter Zolomon also known as Zoom is an extremely powerful Villain and mysterious Metahuman Speedster from Earth 2. He was created by Harrison Wells of Earth 2 when the S.T.A.R. Labs's Particle Accelerator in Central City was turned on and exploded creating Metahumans such as himself and many others.

Barry Allen also known as Savitar is The 1st Metahuman Speedster known in The Multiverse to have ever connect to The Speed Force known to many other Speedsters as The God of Speed

Clifford DeVoe also known as The Thinker is a Metahuman Villain of The Flash from Earth 1. He has extreme knowledge and he is the smartest man ever to live.

Orlin Dwyer also known as Cicada is an extremely powerful and dangerous Metahuman. He's currently hunting his fellow Metahumans one by one killing them since he blames them for all the pain they have caused him and his family in their lives essentially destroying his family resulting his family dying.

Ever since flashpoint, the Timeline has been a real problem for The Flash as he tends to change it a lot; causing lots of problems for him.
